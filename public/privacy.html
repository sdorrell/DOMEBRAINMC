Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Costwatch Consulting LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use Alan_MCP_bot (the "Application"), a QuickBooks Online integration tool.

1. Information We Access

When you authorize the Application, we access the following QuickBooks Online data as required to provide our services:

  • Company information (name, address, fiscal year, contact details)
  • Financial records (invoices, payments, bills, accounts)
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Product and service items

2. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed from your QuickBooks account is used exclusively to perform the actions you request within the Application. We do not:

  • Sell or share your financial data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Train machine learning models on your financial data
  • Retain your QuickBooks data beyond the duration of your session

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth credentials (refresh tokens) are stored securely in encrypted environment variables on our hosting infrastructure. We do not store copies of your QuickBooks financial records. All data transfers occur over HTTPS/TLS encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

This Application uses the Intuit QuickBooks Online API. Your use of QuickBooks is subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain copies of your QuickBooks financial data. OAuth tokens used for authentication are stored only as long as the Application is connected to your account. You may disconnect the Application at any time via your QuickBooks account settings.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke the Application's access to your QuickBooks data at any time by visiting your QuickBooks Online account under Apps & Integrations. Upon revocation, no further access to your data will occur.

7. Children's Privacy

This Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at scott@cashbuyers.us.
